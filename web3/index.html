Web3

House of Peregrine Genesis Collectible

We have an epic vision at House of Peregrine: to make living internationally more beautiful, connected and sustainable by uniting our global community of internationals in a new way. We believe in producing fewer, high-quality products uniquely curated and designed for and by our members. And we boldly believe that our community will play a critical role in shaping House of Peregrine’s future.

Some call our vision —of building a business by growing a supportive community rather than just another blog or facebook group “too ambitious.” We simply call it good growth, and that’s why we are building House of Peregrine in web3. That’s also why we’re working with local businesses in every city to ensure that all of our activities are as carbon negative as possible and rooted in the local culture and economy.

A Global Shift in How We Build Community

The mechanisms for building a global, connected community have evolved dramatically over the last half decade, and particularly so in the last year. Prior to the emergence of the web, meaningful community was relegated to the physical world. We found community in-person, in our neighborhoods, schools, workplaces, and places of worship.

Change—-But this model is not possible when you land in a new city or takes too long to have maximum impact on your initial few months in a new city. We want our community to be able to go deeper, faster with their new city.

The internet promised us the opportunity to accelerate this by building virtual communities instead, but something very different was happening beneath the surface. Our creativity and community-building became free labor for big platform bosses. Not only did Web2 platforms perpetually plunder our connections, their algorithms sorted us into least common denominator versions of ourselves. We ended up connected but with limits that are ready for a reboot.

We’re transcending these limitations by using digital technology to assemble and connect a global community rooted in living globally, locally and remaining connected even after our time together in each city. We aspire to integrate this global community into a participatory brand-building process, collectively building the future of House of Perergrine as a DAO*. We also want to recognize our early supporters for their unwavering belief in this shared vision. Web3 makes all of this possible, and we’re excited to embark on this adventure together.

*WTF is a DAO? A DAO is community bound together by shared ownership toward a common goal.

On 10.10.2022, we’ll officially launch a limited collection of Digital Member Collectibles in collaboration with Laser 3.14, offered as NFTs. When the shop opens these Digital Member Badges grant access to member-exclusive content, perks and prices at houseofperegrine.com.

One of the most important benefits of the Digital Member Badge is visibility into the co-creation process – this is a crucial part of our vision for integrating the community into House of Pergrines’s growth. Usually companies are more top down which can be a reckless gamble backed by natural capital. We’ll work together to create a more deliberate and less destructive approach to meeting the needs of our members and supporting the local economy.

We believe true community consists of direct human connection, and we plan to play a vital role in facilitating that. We’ve already started with online gatherings, but the last thing any of us need is more time spent in the metaverse as amazing as it can be when in a city that you love. IRL activities— from underground parties to co-creating products to contributing to global mobility charities and group web3 learning— have always been at the heart of our imagined House of Peregrine community.

Finally – at some point in the not-so-distant future, your Digital Membership collectible will function as a passport to other web3 communities, both online and off. We’ll build out this cross-functionality with the community to deepen House of Peregrine’s collective value proposition, and we’re excited to open in new cities across the world.

Existing House of Peregrine members will receive an exclusive Laser 3.14 x House of Peregrine Membership Collectible as part of our Genesis launch. For those new to web3 – we’re looking forward to supporting you in onboarding. Soon, we’ll share curated resources for getting started in web3, including how to set up your wallet, best practices for wallet security, and, of course, how to mint your Genesis Collectible.

The Future of Deep Growth

We believe that our community centered model can facilitate a contraction in material production AND an expansion of economic value for everyone who touches our brand. If we’re successful, our good growth foundation will unlock a future of deep growth that benefits us all. We’re ready to build that future and we’re excited to do this in deep collaboration with our community of global adventurers.
